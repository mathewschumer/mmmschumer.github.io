Mat's Record Store

"Your neighborhood music shop"

Mat's Record store is a haven for music lovers in College Park, Maryland. As soon as you step inside, you're transported into a world of cool records and vintage tunes. The shelves are lined with an impressive collection of vinyl records from all genres and eras, ranging from classic rock to contemporary indie. The store has a laid-back vibe, with friendly staff always happy to recommend new records or chat about their favorite bands. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your vinyl journey, Mat's Record store is the perfect place to find your next favorite album.
