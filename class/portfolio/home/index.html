About Me

I am a writer, photographer and broadcaster based in College Park, Maryland and hailing originally from Newtown, Connecticut.

After completing my high school education, I began studying journalism at a few community colleges in Connecticut before settling at Western Connecticut State University. I graduated from WCSU with honors in 2022 and received a Bachelor’s Degree in Journalism. Throughout my studies, I pursued several different extracirriculars including an internship with the university’s public relations department and the campus radio station, of which I was the general manager for a year.

I am currently pursuing my Master’s Degree in Journalism at the University of Maryland’s Phillip Merrill College of Journalism. Concurrently, I write local interest stories for the College Park Here and Now community newspaper and produce music-focused content and concert photography for Surviving the Golden Age.
